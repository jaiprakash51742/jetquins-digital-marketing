HOW DOES SEO WORK WITH GOOGLE?.

SEO is much simpler than the agencies make it out to be.
The Google search algorithm talks about just two simple elements: RELEVANCE and AUTHORITY.
Jetquins Travels professionals focus on these elements to provide the highest return on investment for our clients.

RELEVENCE :

Think of Google as a matching algorithm
Assuming an equal amount of authority as your competitors, the web page that adequately fits a given keyword will rank higher than web pages that only fit a partial keyword.

AUTHORITY :

Quantity + Quality of links to your website
Google trusts thousands of websites to be highly authoritative. When these websites link to you, they contribute some of that trust and authority.

FAQ's.

  • 1. What does Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Mean?
    SEO simply means, optimizing the content and structural layout of your website for better ranking on search engine pages. When executed adequately, SEO improves your website’s presence on Google’s SERP when a user searches for a certain keyword or term. The purpose here is to generate an appealing return on investment for your business caused by driving a higher number of potential buyers to your website.
  • 2. What are SEO services?

  • 3. Can People Find My Site On Google Right Now?

    The most suitable method to test this is to go to Google and type in “site:[mydomain.com]” and check if your website pops up. For example, to check if Intergrowth was indexed, I would type “site:inter-growth.co” into search engines. If your website doesn’t pop up after researching this way, then your website isn’t on Google right now. To rectify this issue, you must add your website to Search Console and submit your sitemap after verifying your site.
    Struggling to find your sitemap? Try typing “[mydomain.com]/sitemap.xml” into your browser. In most instances, this will pull up your sitemap, or at least redirect you to your sitemap page. If this doesn’t work, then get in touch with our SEO agency in Pune asap! as your website has a lot of work to do.

  • 4. What Parts of SEO Should I Focus on First?

    Our best suggestion here would be to consult with an SEO expert in Pune and acquire their opinion. That being said, a good general rule of thumb is to check your website’s domain authority. In most circumstances, if your website’s authority is less than 40, you need to focus on the Authority side of SEO (i.e. getting more qualified websites to link to you). If your website’s authority is greater than 70, you must focus on the Relevancy side of SEO (setting up a proper SEO foundation).
    If your website falls somewhere in the between, then do a search for 5 of your biggest competitors, and compare your domain authority to theirs. If your domain authority is higher than theirs, then it might be the finest option to focus on Relevancy. If your domain authority is lower than theirs, then it might be the best option to focus on Authority.
    Finding it hard to decide which makes more sense for your business? Schedule a call with our SEO agency in Pune and let us set you in the right direction.
