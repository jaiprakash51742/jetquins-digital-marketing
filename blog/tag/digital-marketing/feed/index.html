Fair Play: The traditional methods of marketing have been a boon for big businesses who can shell out the money for high profile and prime time ads. A well thought out marketing strategy can easily increase the site traffic by leaps and bounds. You also don’t need to wait for the statistics to find out if your strategy worked. So, the business owners would know what worked and what didn’t in almost no time and they could change their marketing strategy.
